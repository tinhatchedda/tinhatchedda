The Chedda Files – Vol. 1: The Incident Logs

These recordings were never supposed to exist. The government labeled the event “Project MF Chedda” and buried the footage under six layers of clearance and one very confused intern. But the signal leaks—through static, through memes, through the kind of Wi-Fi that smells like burnt popcorn and paranoia.

What you’re about to see are the recovered fragments: five visual glitches left behind when Greg and Bigfoot cracked open a portal with a joint, a microwave, and the world’s most dangerous cheese.

Welcome to Tin Hat Chedda. Reality melts at 420 °F.

File 01 — The Chedda Incident

Somewhere between midnight hunger and poor judgment, Greg finds a foil pouch stamped in red: PROJECT MF CHEDDA — DO NOT CONSUME. Bigfoot tells him to chill. Greg lights a joint instead. The microwave hums like it’s remembering the future. The air thickens. The cheese glows.

File 02 — The Pull

The cheese doesn’t melt—it detonates softly, like a star folding in on itself. The cabin forgets how to be solid. Greg and Bigfoot are lifted, laughing, screaming, praying—it’s all the same sound now. Somewhere in the echo, reality asks: “You sure you’re ready for this?”

File 03 — Clockland Arrival

They land in a meadow of broken timepieces and sentient mushrooms. The sky blinks. Pyramids breathe. A TV floats by and changes the channel to their faces. Greg laughs. Bigfoot keeps eating.

File 04 — Prophecy of the Shroom

A sagely mushroom awakens and speaks in clocks: “Clockwise to forget… counterclockwise to feed the portal.” The text spirals out of its grin. Greg nods like he understands. He does not.

File 05 — The Glitch Return

Greg’s reflection blinks first. Bigfoot is buffering. Reality stutters and saves as ‘Untitled Final 2.’ The TV coughs, the portal purrs, the cheese smiles. They are back. Or they never left.
